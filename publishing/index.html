Publishing Portal

Get City data published on the data portal

So you want to publish data on SF OpenData? We have just a couple of steps to get you started.

  1. Sign up for SF OpenData Account (if you haven't already)
  2. Download and complete the Publisher's Packet More Help
    Here's where more stuff goes
  3. Submit your packet

Someone from the open data team will follow up with you shortly to guide you through the rest of the process. Don't worry, it's not complicated. Think of us as your data sherpas guiding you through your journey to published data.
