Office Hours

If you have a specific question about a program you're using, or need guidance about how to solve a particular challenge you've run into, Data Academy Office Hours is the place for you! 

We are currently offering Office Hours in Microsoft PowerBI, so read below to determine if your issue could be solved during an Office Hours session. Just follow the instructions to sign up for a time slot, then sit down for a 1-on-1 session to work through the roadblock you're having trouble with. See you soon at office hours! 

Have a question about Data Academy or other programs/ concepts covered in a Data Academy course? Feel free to reach out to DataAcademy@sfgov.org.
